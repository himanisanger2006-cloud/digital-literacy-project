College Students' Checklist for Responsible Social Media Use 
? Do's 
Before you post, think about how your content might impact your reputation both now and down the road. 
Protect your privacy by changing your privacy settings and refraining from disclosing private information. 
Be courteous and upbeat; have productive discussions and show kindness to others. 
Check information before disseminating it to prevent rumours or false information from spreading. 
Maintain a healthy balance between your online and offline time by giving academics, sleep, and in-person relationships top priority. 
? Avoid 
Don’t overshare personal details – avoid posting your location, schedules, or private matters. 
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or harassment – negative behavior can have serious consequences. 
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content – it can harm your academic and career opportunities. 
Don’t believe everything you see – social media can present misleading or false information.
